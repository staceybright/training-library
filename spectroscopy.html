Basic Spectroscopy¶

The spectroscopy training document gives an overview of spectrographs in general and the basics of spectroscopy. Also included are exercises that will introduce you to using the data from HST’s spectrographs–COS and STIS.

There is a jupyter notebook version of the training that is designed to be worked through interactively in group training.

There are also slides that give a high-level overview of COS, STIS, and MAMA detectors.
